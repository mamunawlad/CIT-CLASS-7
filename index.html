5 Reasons You Should Hire a Freelance
Real Estate Photographer

By Fiverr Team | April 26, 2023

Did you know that 97% of homebuyerssearch the internet for a new house?
In the digital age, a great picture says more than a thousand words — it can
also put more money in your client's pocket.
Listings with more positive attention get more showings, maximizing your
client's chance of selling for top dollar. When your home seller's happiness
is on the line, it pays to invest in a freelance real estate photographer to drum
up the best real estate marketing.
If you're wondering whether a pro is worth the investment, here are five
reasons to hire one.

1. Your Client Gets the Best Photos Possible

When you hire a freelance photographer from Fiverr, you can get the best
possible listing photos on MLS, Zillow, and other popular online directories.
Photographers are experts in the best practices of real estate photography,
implementing the right techniques to make your listing shine. They know how
to take the right angles to make a small room look the largest and most appealing.
On top of that, photographers have pro-level editing software to get just
the right look for each room of the house so that your listing stands out from
the others. These won't look photoshopped or fake but just naturally enhanced
to make a grand first impression. If there is something intrusive, like a
personal family effect in the photo, your photographer can edit it to make it impersonal,
which helps the buyer envision the house as a neutral space.


2. You Get the Best Value for Your Investment

Professional photography equipment is expensive. To take excellent photos,
you need professional photography gear and vast knowledge of taking excellent
photos. High-grade equipment with different types of lenses is critical to
taking sharp, clear images that genuinely make an impression. Taking ordinary
photos on your smartphone simply won't deliver the same results.

Therefore, hiring a photographer who already has thousands of dollars worth
of pro gear and a deep knowledge of best practices is a valuable investment for
your marketing material. It's like having access to the best camera without
buying it.

3. Houses With Good Photos Sell Quicker

When it comes down to the wire, your client cares about getting top dollar
for selling their house. Recent statistics show that listings with high dynamic range
images sell 50% faster and have 118% more online views. If you also take
the extra step to include aerial photos with a drone, the listing typically
sells 68% faster. These stats alone make pro photography a worthwhile
investment.

4. You Save Time by Delegating Photography

Marketing a listing is no small feat. Not only do you need to get great images,
but you also need to prepare the MLS listing at the office and ensure you get all
the important information from your client to make the home marketable.
With so much clerical and administrative work, taking care of the photography
yourself can take longer to get your listing live.
Delegating the photography will expedite your marketing process with better results.
This is especially helpful for larger, busy real estate teams that require all
hands on deck to market, show, and represent clients.

5. You'll Get a Major Advantage in a Competitive Industry

Though more agents are investing in pro photography than ever, only 35% of agents
hire professional photographers. This means that simply having professional photos
will give you an advantage over 65% of comparative listings. This also
means that amateur photos can get lost in the crowd and make your listing
look less valuable. Getting the right photos up the first time can be
a game changer.

Every real estate agent should have a freelance real estate photographer on their team.
